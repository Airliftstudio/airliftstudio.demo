Why should I support multiple languages on my Airbnb website?

While most of your guests may speak perfectly fine English, giving guests the option to view your website in their local language gives them a more premium experience. But the main reason we recommend adding multiple languages is that it increases your chance to show up in search results when potential guests are searching in their own language. We recommend to include the local language in your area and the top languages spoken by your usual guests. You may also add languages to target new countries that you want to reach guests from.

With so many options out there, creating a website can feel overwhelming — from hiring freelancers by the hour to navigating subscription based website builders. We believe we offer the perfect balance: a beautiful, effective site at an affordable fixed price, completely hassle-free. Our full-service approach means you can sit back and relax while we handle everything. Plus, we offer a free demo so you’ll know exactly what you’re getting before you commit.

How should I choose a domain name for my Airbnb website?

If your property already has a strong branded name (e.g., Villa Zori), you can use it as your domain name. If not, consider using a keyword that reflects your property and location, such as junglevillabali.com or balisunrisecabin.com. We recommend choosing a .com domain, as it’s the most common, recognizable, and SEO-friendly. You can quickly check availability by typing your desired domain into your browser. If you’re unsure what to choose, we’ll suggest a great domain name for you as part of your free demo.

What happens after my hands-off package expires?

With our hands-off package, domain name registration and hosting are included, along with other features. Your package covers the first year of up-time; to keep your website online and prevent others from taking your domain, you’ll need to renew it before it expires. You can manage all of this in your Cloudflare account, renewals are on a yearly basis and pricing may increase over time at Cloudflares discretion. Domain names can not be bought permanently. You can secure your domain and up-time for up to 10 years and avoid the hassle of yearly renewals. At any time—during or after your package—you’re free to move your website to another provider.
