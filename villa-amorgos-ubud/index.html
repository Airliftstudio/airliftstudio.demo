A Peaceful Escape in Ubud's Heart

Welcome to Villa Amorgos I, a cozy villa located in Ubud, Bali. Ideal for a relaxing escape, this 1-bedroom property is designed for up to 3 guests and offers comfort, simplicity, and a private setting surrounded by nature.

The villa includes air conditioning throughout the house and a private swimming pool with the famous swing. Guests can enjoy floating breakfast service, in-villa massages, and complete privacy in this jungle paradise just minutes from Ubud center.
