Discover Historic Sebatu

Why This Location is Perfect

Experience Bali as it was in the mid-20th century near the historic and culturally rich village of Sebatu. Our village is one of only a handful that has sacred water springs and baths called Gunung Kawi, which has been inhabited for over 1,000 years.

  • 1000-year old sacred water temple
  • Famous wood and stone carvers
  • Traditional Balinese gamelan musicians
  • Curative waterfall nearby
  • Free shuttle to Ubud cultural center
